How to use StudyMate

  • Quick vs Detailed: use the switch at the top. Quick is brief (3–5 steps); Detailed is deeper (6–9 steps with examples).
  • Take/Upload/Type: choose how to send your question. We run OCR for photos.
  • Read Aloud: after an explanation appears, use the toolbar to hear it.
  • History: your questions/solutions are saved locally on this device only.
  • Sketch: draw highlights on screen, then tap Done to close.
  • Theme/Font: customize from the top controls and bottom-left buttons.
